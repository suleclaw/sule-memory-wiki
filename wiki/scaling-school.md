# Scaling School

**Status:** Audit complete ✅ — 26 career tracks verified
**Source:** scaling-school.com

---

## Overview

Scaling School is an online learning platform with structured career tracks. Dami audited the platform to evaluate its use as a resource and potential partnership for GetRoleUp.

---

## Audit Result

26 verified career tracks across:
- Frontend development
- Backend development
- DevOps / Platform engineering
- Data science
- Product management
- Design

Each track provides a curated path through courses and projects — structured learning vs. scattered tutorials.

---

## Relevance to GetRoleUp

GetRoleUp (see [[getroleup]]) has a similar mission: structured learning paths for career growth. The Scaling School audit confirmed the 26-track library and provided insights into how to structure a learning platform effectively.
